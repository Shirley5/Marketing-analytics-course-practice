Our Story

Born from the Borderland

Sun City Coffee Roasters started in a small warehouse off Montana Avenue in 2018. We believed El Paso deserved access to the same world-class specialty coffee enjoyed in major coastal cities — without the pretension.

  • Roasted in small batches, every week, right here in El Paso
  • Direct trade relationships with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala
  • Free shipping on all subscription orders to the 915 area
  • Proud supporter of UTEP's business school and local hospitality industry

From Farm to Cup

How We Roast

Every bag you receive has gone through a meticulous four-step journey before it reaches your kitchen.

01
🌱
Direct Sourcing

We travel to origin farms annually to taste and select green coffee lots, paying above Fair Trade minimums directly to farmers.

02
🔬
Green Grading

On arrival in El Paso, every lot is cupped and scored by our certified Q-Grader. Only 80+ SCA-scored coffees make the cut.

03
🔥
Small-Batch Roasting

Roasted in 15kg batches on our Probat roaster every Tuesday and Friday. Orders ship within 48 hours of roasting for peak freshness.

04
📦
Sealed & Shipped

Packed in nitrogen-flushed, valve-sealed bags that preserve freshness for up to 6 months — though they rarely last that long.
